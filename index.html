Major Project Part B


In part B we did our paper prototyping and I was the observer for majority of the part.Some of the key observations I made about the users are the following
  • User seemed a bit confused at first about the context and goal of the program (required some explanation from the facilitator)
  • Tell the user what they’re looking for, give more context on the game and subject
  • Start page / home page
  • Instructions for how the game works
  • Possibly have a timer to have an urgency in the gameplay
  • Have question either given at the start or have accessible at all times
  • Let the user answer the question as soon as they have the information
After our prototyping we immediately began our development of our website and incorporating the feedback that was given to us. Which were basically the issues I observed in the dot points above. Each team member was assigned a specific task , I was responsible for the the front end development ( HTML & CSS). I got the coding completed in time, however, some alterations needed to be made at the end hence not meeting our personal deadlines for the progress report although we did manage to meet the part be deadline for submission of our report.



It is in this section we realised some of major problems with ourprototype. such as lack of direction, lack of context, lack of urgency in the game, the answer system,

Course Reflection

Looking at my journey throughout DECO1800, I can confidently say I have learned many usefull skills which will come handyin the future.

Firstly, one of the biggest things that I learnt in this course is how to work as a part of a team. It was a very real world experience. I learnt to rely and trust on the rest of my teammates, resolve team conflicts and conceptualize and brainstorm idea within the team.

Secondly, I have a much deeper understanding of the design process, and how a developer will interact with clients and team members to synthesize and final product.
Looking back I one thing I would say is that I will need to manage my time more efficiently and take in count potential mess ups and failures.

I believe that DECO1800 was overall an effective course, in the way that it allowed me to experience design process firsthand and learn about real world process.
